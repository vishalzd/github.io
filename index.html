Carbon Credit Lifecycle

From Project Concept to Carbon Credits

Understanding the complete journey of carbon project development

01

Feasibility Assessment

Initial project screening, eligibility check, and methodology selection to determine carbon credit potential.

Duration: 2-4 weeks
02

Project Design & PDD

Development of comprehensive Project Design Document including baseline calculations, additionality demonstration, and monitoring plan.

Duration: 2-4 months
03

Validation

Independent third-party validation by accredited Validation & Verification Body (VVB) to verify project compliance.

Duration: 3-6 months
04

Registration

Project listing and registration with Verra VCS or Gold Standard registry after successful validation.

Duration: 1-2 months
05

Monitoring & Implementation

Ongoing project implementation with continuous monitoring, data collection, and reporting as per approved monitoring plan.

Duration: Continuous
06

Verification

Periodic third-party verification of emission reductions achieved, typically conducted annually or bi-annually.

Duration: 2-4 months
07

Credit Issuance

Verified carbon credits are issued by the registry and transferred to project owner's account.

Duration: 1-2 months
08

Monetization

Carbon credits are sold to buyers in voluntary carbon markets, generating revenue for project owners.

Duration: Ongoing
